Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian playback singer and music composer. The recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award and seven Filmfare Awards, he has recorded songs in several Indian languages, predominantly Hindi and Bengali. In 2014 Singh received 2 nominations in Filmfare Awards for songs "Mast Magan" and "Suno Na Sangemarmar" respectively. He won the GiMA award for the song "Muskurane". He also received several nominations for songs like "Palat", "Humdard" and many more.
